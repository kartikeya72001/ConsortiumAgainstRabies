CARCON 2014 - 1st Feb 2014 - hosted at AIIMS Jodhpur !!!

The 4th National Conference of Consortium Against Rabies was held on 1st Feb 2014 at AIIMS Jodhpur organized by Department of Community Medicine and Family Medicine.

Many departments and organizations came together for this grand event. Indian Council for Medical Research, National Center for Disease Control, IIPH Gandhinagar, Association of Family Physicians of India, IPHA Rajasthan Chapter and Veterinary department of Rajasthan Government and many other medical colleges from various states of India joined hands to combat the issue of rabies control in India.

Dr. Pankaja Ravi Raghav and Dr. Pankaj Bhardwaj and their team put in the best of efforts to bring together both local and national experts for the deliberations. Watch out this space for the proceedings of the conference... to be posted soon.
