CARCON-2020
Updated Guidelines

OBJECTIVES

To serve as a platform for the control of the rabies menace in India. It will evolve into a full fledged struggle against rabies in the country.

Intention

CAR will bring the best minds in the country for research and information dissemination in the field of rabies.

Ambition

To undertake national epidemiological surveillance to study the true figure of rabies deaths in our country so that we know the real extent of this disease.

Grail

To lay emphasis on the practice of proper post-exposure treatment procedure and increase in serum and vaccine coverage.
