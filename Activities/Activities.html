CARCON 2017 - 4th and 5th March 2015 - Integral Institute of Medical Sciences, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh - First Announceent

The 5th National Conference of Consortium Against Rabies was held on 4th and 5th March 2017 at Integral Institute of Medical Sciences, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. Log on to: www.carcon2017.com for registration, abstract submission and other details.

CARCON 2013 - New Delhi 20th April 2013 - A huge success

The department of Community Medicine, UCMS and GTB Hospital, University of Delhi,organized the 3rd National Conference of Consortium Against Rabies in association with Consortium Against Rabies on 20th April 2013. It was attended by more than 200 delegates from all over India and there were presentations by experienced researchers in the field of Rabies. The speakers were from prime institutes like National Centers for Disease Control, Public Health Foundation of India and other academic institutes like AIIMS, Rishikesh, UCMS, Delhi. The Director of Health Services, Govt of NCT of Delhi inaugurated the conference. The conference was a huge success.
